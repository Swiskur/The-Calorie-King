Hello and welcome to Calorie King, the web app where you can keep track of your calorie intake! Our main goal here is to make sure you have all of the necessary tools to make goals, calculate your calories and become the best version of yourself. Below is the features and how to use the app. Welcome and we hope you enjoy!


How to use Calorie King

  • Go to our Search and get calories for the specific foods you have eaten
  • Enter your daily calorie goal
  • Enter your calorie totals from each meal, including protein and fat
  • Keep track of your daily totals on the results page!
