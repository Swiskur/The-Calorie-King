Hello and welcome to Calorie King, the web app where you can keep track of your calorie intake and calories burned! Our main goal here is to make sure you have all of the necessary tools to make goals, calculate your calories/calories burned and become the best version of yourself. Below is the features and how to use the app. Welcome and we hope you enjoy!


How to use Calorie King

  • Go to our Search and get calories for the specific foods you have eaten
  • Then go to one of our new features the burner search where you can search activities and see how many calories you've burned
  • Enter your daily calorie goal
  • Enter your calorie totals from each meal, including protein and fat
  • Enter your total calories burned into our calorie burner calculator
  • Keep track of your daily totals on the calorie results and burner results page!
